Pole Erection Machine

A Pole Erection Machine is a tractor-operated application designed especially for rural electrification work. It is used to plant big plants and to erect electricity and solar poles. The pit designs and dimensions can be customized as per pit design

Fasteners for use in pre cast conrete/concrete foundations

Fasteners for use in precast concrete or concrete foundations are usually made of stainless steel. They are used to attach various components to the foundation such as walls, beams and other structural elements.

Concrete Boundary wall cement compound walls

A concrete boundary wall is constructed using concrete blocks and bonded together using mortar of required proportion. This is also known as the RCC compound wall. Because of the involvement of concrete blocks, this type of boundary wall offers great strength
